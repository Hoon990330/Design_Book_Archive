This website functions as a design book archive that introduces famous book designs created by graphic designers and explains the concept and intent behind each work. It aims to highlight the artistic and conceptual value of book design.

SHV Holdings

Irma Boom’s SHV Holdings (1996) is a 4 kg, 2,136-page corporate book created for SHV Holdings' centennial. Limited to 4,500 copies and never sold, it challenges traditional book design with no page numbers, index, or chapters. Layered text, varied typography, and diverse paper textures make it an immersive, nonlinear experience. Boom’s experimental approach turns the book into a sculptural, interactive object, redefining print design.

Artist: Irma Boom

Year of Production: 1996
