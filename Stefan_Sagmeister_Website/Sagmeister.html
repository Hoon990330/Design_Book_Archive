This website functions as a design book archive that introduces famous book designs created by graphic designers and explains the concept and intent behind each work. It aims to highlight the artistic and conceptual value of book design.

Made You Look

Stefan Sagmeister – Made You Look (2001) is a visually striking monograph showcasing his unconventional, provocative approach to graphic design. Blending commercial and personal projects, it highlights his experimental use of typography, bold imagery, and conceptual storytelling. Known for pushing boundaries, Sagmeister integrates hand-crafted elements and unexpected materials to create emotionally engaging work. Made You Look documents his influential career while exploring design’s power to provoke thought and evoke strong reactions.

Artist: Stefan Sagmeister

Year of Production: 2001
